About SHEENA

SHEENA is from Santiago City, Isabela, Philippines. Her favourite colours are black and purple. She enjoys dancing, singing, browsing social media and eating. Her favourite SHA subject is dancing. She can bend her thumb. Sheena started dancing when she was 8 years old. She joined a dance group called M1T (Most One Ted Dance Group). The M1T then joined the Star Hunt Grand Audition. Sheena was a part of PBB Otso batch 3 just like Gwen and was top 7/8. Sheena loves sweets and bonchon. Her idol is Ken San Jose. Life Motto: ”Never give up. Dont ever think of giving up.”
